📋 Applications

Where to Apply — Complete Guide

Every license, API, and registration you need. Exact steps, costs, and links.

🏛
SECP — Company Registration
Pakistan · Legal Foundation
  • 1Go to SECP eZfile portal (eservices.secp.gov.pk)
  • 2Register as Pvt. Ltd. (min 2 directors, PKR 100k capital)
  • 3Submit MOA + AOA + CNIC copies
  • 4Pay registration fee online
  • 5Get Certificate of Incorporation (3-5 days)
🔗 eservices.secp.gov.pk
💰 PKR 12,000-25,000 · ⏱ 3-5 days
📋
FBR — NTN + STRN
Pakistan · Tax Registration
  • 1Go to iris.fbr.gov.pk (FBR IRIS portal)
  • 2Register company with SECP certificate
  • 3Apply for NTN (National Tax Number)
  • 4Apply for Sales Tax Registration (STRN)
  • 5Activate withholding tax status
🔗 iris.fbr.gov.pk
💰 Free · ⏱ 3-7 days
🔒
PVARA — VASP License
Pakistan · Virtual Assets Act 2026
  • 1Contact PVARA at pvara.gov.pk (new authority)
  • 2Apply for NOC first (prerequisite)
  • 3Apply for Categories 3,4,5,8,9 licenses
  • 4Submit AML/CFT policy + business plan
  • 5Appoint MLRO + compliance officer
  • 6Capital adequacy proof (PKR 500M+)
🔗 pvara.gov.pk
💰 PKR 2-10M per category · ⏱ 6-12 months
📡
